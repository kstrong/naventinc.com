The Jungle Bots Adventure will take guests on a thrilling journey into the world of Metallica robotics animals in this classic futurist’s world. The innovative incorporation of a six passenger’s lifeboat system with evocative environments, state of the art animatronics and a thrilling ending promise to deliver an unforgettable adventure for every guest.

From the moment visitors enter the Jungle Bots Temple Visitor Center, there are ominous sounds of thunder echoing through the overhead skies. Undaunted, each guest boards a life boat for an adventure on a lost island starting when the boat suddenly appear to break ups on a submerged reef, tossing the guest into a underwater craven without any power just floating down stream. After exiting the craven we enter the jungle still floating down tree line river, when all of a sudden the guest are nearly trampled to death by a strange robotic creatures just out of reach.
