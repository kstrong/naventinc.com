Wax figures (life-sized, genuine three-dimensional representations) of the most celebrated and important people of History and Hollywood are an intricate art form. Each star portrayed is life-size with uncanny characteristics & likeness. Facial features, body & bone structure are recreated precisely according to the star own image. Original looking costumes & studio props can be created to ensure authenticity. In order to enhance the enjoyment of visitors, special lighting, sound effects & animation can be used throughout the museum.
